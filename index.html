AT FULFILMENT CHAPEL

We are committed to the physical, emotional and spiritual welfare and safety of all people, particularly within its own community.
Our aim is that everyone within our church will be able to safely participate and contribute in appropriate ministry programs, have every opportunity to grow and flourish, and that they will be able to safely report any incidents of abuse or safety concerns.
We will respond appropriately to all reports in the interest of all those involved.
To build and maintain a safe community for our children and vulnerable adults, we are committed to ensuring that:


1. All leaders are appropriately appointed, screened, and trained (see ‘Safe Ministry Policy and Process’ section below);

2. All programs are led and run in a safe and appropriate manner; and

3. There are clear procedures in place for dealing with incident reports and safety concerns.
